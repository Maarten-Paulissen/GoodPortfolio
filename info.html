About Me

Hi, I'm Maarten Paulissen, and I’m excited to share a bit about myself with you. I began my journey as a kindergarten teacher, a role that taught me the importance of creativity, patience, and connection. Over time, I developed a strong interest in computers and programming, sparked by my curiosity about how technology works behind the scenes. As I grew older, this passion only deepened, leading me to explore and eventually transition into the world of web development. Today, I’m proud to call myself a frontend developer, combining my love for problem-solving and design to create seamless, user-friendly digital experiences. Thank you for visiting, and feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or learn more about my journey!
