Project 2: Hanging man

Hanging Man is a classic word-guessing game where players try to figure out a hidden word by guessing one letter at a time. Each incorrect guess brings the player closer to completing the "hanging man" drawing. Here's how it works: A word is chosen, and the player sees blank spaces representing each letter. The player guesses letters to fill in the blanks. Each wrong guess adds a part to the hanging man. The game ends when the word is guessed correctly or the hanging man is fully drawn. It's a fun and challenging way to test vocabulary and problem-solving skills!
